    Gen Ai Engineer
    Capgemini
    3.7
    47311 Reviews

    Job description

    Key Responsibilities:

    • Design and implement generative AI models using LLMs, GANs, VAEs, and diffusion models.
    • Fine-tune and optimize pre-trained models (e.g., GPT, LLaMA, Claude) for specific business use cases.
    • Develop and maintain scalable pipelines for training, evaluation, and deployment of generative models.
    • Integrate generative models into production systems using APIs and cloud services.
    • Collaborate with data scientists, product managers, and software engineers to define project goals and deliverables.
    • Monitor model performance and continuously improve accuracy, efficiency, and safety.
    • Stay up to date with the latest research and advancements in generative AI and machine learning.

    Required Skills & Qualifications:

    • Bachelor's or Masters degree in Computer Science, AI, Machine Learning, or a related field.
    • 3+ years of experience in machine learning, with at least 1–2 years focused on generative models.
    • Proficiency in Python and ML frameworks like PyTorch or TensorFlow.
    • Experience with LLMs, RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation), and vector databases (e.g., FAISS, Pinecone).
    • Familiarity with MLOps tools (e.g., MLflow, Weights & Biases) and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).
    • Strong understanding of deep learning architectures and training techniques.
    • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure) for model deployment and scaling.
